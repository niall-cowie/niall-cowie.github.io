Implications of AI in Education

How will AI Interplay with Education and What are Potential Consequences?

Research Question

Will AI meaningfully enhance educational outcomes, or will it undermine human-centered education and human agency in a digital age?

Why It Matters

As AI tools become increasingly embedded in everyday learning, they promise efficiency and customization, yet may also encourage passivity, overreliance, and widen educational inequalities. Understanding their widespread impact is essential for shaping education systems that ought to be equitable and human-centered.

Initial Conclusions

  • AI has the potential to democratize certain types of academic support
  • While AI can improve efficiency and offer clarity, its impact on deeper learning remains unclear
  • AI may change the student-teacher relationship
